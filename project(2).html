2. GREAT WALL OF CHINA

THE GREAT WALL OF CHINA IS A SERIES OF FORTIFICATIONS BUILT OVER CENTURIES,PRIMARILY AS A MILITARY DEFENSE AGAINST INVASIONS FROM THE NORTH. IT IS THE WOLRD'S LONGEST MAN-MADE STRUCTURE, STRECTCHING FOR APPROXIMATELY 21,196 KILOMETERS( 13,170 MILES ). CONSTRUCTION BEGAN AS EARLY AS THE 7TH CENTURY BCE,AND DIFFERENT DYNASTIES, MOST NOTABLY THE MING DYNASTY, EXPANDED AND REINFORCED IT WITH MATERIALS LIKE RAMMED EARTH, BRICK , AND STONE. TODAY , IT SERVES AS A POWERFUL CULTURAL SYMBOL AND A MAJOR TOURIST ATTRACTION.

CONSTRUCTION AND PURPOSE

PRIMARY PURPOSE : THE WALL'S MAIN FUNCTION WAS DEFENSE AGAINST NOMADIC GROUPS AND INVASIONS FROM THE NORTH. IT ALSO SERVED AS A MEANS OF BORDER CONTROL, REGULATING TRADE ALONG ROUTES LIKE THE SILK ROAD, AND CONTROLLING MIGRATION.

CONSTRUCTION TIMELINE : CONSTRUCTION OCCURRED OVER MANY CENTURIES, WITH THE FIRST WALLS BUILT IN THE 7 TH CENTURY BCE. THE QIN DYNASTY CONNECTED EARLIER WALLS, AND THE MING DYNASTY (1368-1644 CE) BUILT THE MOST WELL-KNOWN AND SUBSTANTIAL SECTIONS.

MATERIALS AND STRUCTURE : EARLY SECTIONS WERE MADE OF RAMMED EARTH, BUT LATER PARTS, ESPECIALLY DURING THE MING DYNASTY, USED BRICK AND STONE. THE STRUCTURE INCLUDES WATCHTOWERS, FORTRESSES, AND PASSES ALONG ITS LENGTH.

DIMENSIONS AND FACTS

LENGTH : THE TOTAL LENGTH IS APPROXIMATELY 21,196.18 KM (13,170.70 MILES.)

HEIGHT : THE AVERAGE HEIGTH IS AROUND 7.8 METERS (25.6 FEET), THOUGH IT CAN REACH UP TO 14 METERS (46 FEET) IN SOME PLACES.

WIDTH : THE AVERAGE WIDTH IS ABOUT 6.5 METERS (21.3 FEET).
